FAQ


How can I donate to help flood victims in Noakhali and Feni?

You can donate directly through our website using various payment methods such as mobile banking, credit card, or bank transfer. Every contribution, big or small, helps provide essential aid such as food, shelter, and medical supplies to the affected communities in Noakhali and Feni.

What type of assistance is being provided to the flood victims?

Donations are being used to provide immediate relief such as food, clean water, medicines, shelter, and basic necessities to those affected by the floods in Noakhali and Feni. We are also working to support long-term recovery efforts, such as rebuilding homes and providing financial assistance to families who have lost their livelihoods.

How will my donation help the injured people from the July 2024 uprising?

Your donation will directly support medical treatment, rehabilitation, and psychological support for individuals injured during the July 2024 uprising in Bangladesh. We are working with hospitals, health clinics, and local organizations to ensure victims receive the care they need.

What can I do to help people suffering from the winter in Bangladesh?

Donations are being used to provide warm clothing, blankets, food, and shelter to vulnerable communities suffering from the harsh winter conditions. Your contributions will help ensure that those in need are protected from the extreme cold, especially in rural areas where winter hardships are most severe.
